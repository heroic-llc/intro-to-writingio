# CLAUDE.md - Project Instructions

## Course Overview
Intro to writingIO - An onboarding video series for new users and potential customers.

## Audience
- New writingIO users during onboarding
- Potential customers evaluating the platform
- Anyone curious about writingIO

## Tone and Style
- Friendly and welcoming
- Direct and one-on-one (speaking to the viewer)
- Positive language (avoid negative framing)
- Professional but approachable

## Video Script Guidelines
- Brand name is "writingIO" (not "Writing IO" or "writing.io")
- Spell out numbers and acronyms for speech (e.g., "A I" not "AI")
- Scripts must be under 5000 characters for HeyGen
- Amanda is the Chief Learning Officer and narrator
- First video discloses AI tool usage

## Key Messages
- writingIO helps you use AI effectively
- Over 100,000 individuals including professionals at Microsoft, Shopify, NASA
- Learning platform + powerful AI tools in one place
- Real humans behind everything (courses, support, community)
- Easy to get started - a few lessons builds confidence
