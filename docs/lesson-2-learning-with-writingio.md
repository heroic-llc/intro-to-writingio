# Intro to writingIO

## Lesson 2: Learning with writingIO

### Overview
This lesson explores how learning works on writingIO—interactive courses, certifications, and badges to celebrate your progress.

---

### How We Approach Learning

We know you're busy. That's why our courses are:
- **Interactive and practical** - Learn by doing, not just watching
- **Short video lessons** combined with hands-on exercises
- **Designed to stick** - You use AI as you go, so the knowledge stays with you

### Courses and Certifications

We offer a wide range of courses:
- **Quick primers** - Get up to speed fast on specific topics
- **In-depth courses** - Push deeper into areas that matter to you

When you complete a course, you earn a **certificate** that shows you know your stuff:
- Add it to your LinkedIn profile
- Share it with your employer
- Use it for recommended continuing education

### Badges and Recognition

As you progress, you earn badges within writingIO. It feels good to see how far you've come!
